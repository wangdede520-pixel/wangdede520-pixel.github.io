CleanSnap Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-06-07
This App is developed and operated by Wuxi Yijizhili Information Technology Co., Ltd.
Contact Email: wangdede520@outlook.com

CleanSnap (the “App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Wuxi Yijizhili Information Technology Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect or Process

Depending on the features you use, the App may collect or process the following information:

  • Photo Library Permission Information: used to access photos and videos within the permission scope you authorize, so the App can scan, organize, compress, preview, and delete media.
  • Local Media Metadata: such as thumbnails, resolution, creation date, duration, file size, screenshot flag, media type, and existing location metadata embedded in your media (if available and accessible under system permissions), solely for local analysis and display.
  • Subscription Status Information: if you purchase premium features, Apple StoreKit may return subscription status, transaction status, and product identifiers so the App can determine your premium access.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • detect duplicate photos, similar photos, duplicate videos, similar videos, screenshots, and low-quality photos;
  • provide media preview and organization features;
  • perform photo or video compression;
  • delete media from your photo library after your confirmation;
  • verify, restore, and manage your subscription status;
  • improve product stability and user experience.

3. Local Processing

The App is designed to process media analysis primarily on your device. In general, your photos and videos are not uploaded to our servers for scanning and analysis.

Media content that you authorize the App to access is typically processed locally for tasks such as similarity identification, size calculation, preview generation, compression, and other necessary feature-related processing.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your photos or videos without your explicit authorization.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your media content for advertising targeting.
  • We do not access your photo library beyond what is necessary for the App’s features.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following permission:

  • Photos Permission: used for scanning, organizing, compressing, previewing, and deleting photos or videos.

You may revoke or adjust permissions at any time in your iOS device settings. If permission is denied, some features may not work properly.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases and payments in the App are handled by Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your credit card number, Apple ID password, or other payment credentials. Payment processing is governed by Apple’s own policies and terms.

7. Storage and Deletion

During scanning, compression, preview, or related operations, the App may create temporary local cache files on your device in order to complete processing. Such temporary files are generally deleted automatically after processing or cleaned up in subsequent flows.

Media deletion initiated through the App uses the system photo library deletion mechanism, and the final result depends on the system’s actual execution.

8. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App may rely on system or platform services such as:

  • Apple Photos
  • Apple StoreKit
  • Apple system frameworks for media processing and web display

These services are provided by third parties and may be subject to their own privacy policies.

9. Children’s Privacy

If you are a minor, please review this Privacy Policy with your parent or legal guardian before using the App.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to feature changes, legal requirements, or business adjustments. Updated versions will be published on the corresponding page. Continued use of the App after an update means you acknowledge and accept the updated policy.
