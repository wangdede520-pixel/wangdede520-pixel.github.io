清简相册 / CleanSnap

清简相册（CleanSnap）是一款照片与视频整理工具，可用于识别重复内容、相似内容、截图和低质量照片，并提供压缩与清理辅助功能。
CleanSnap is a photo and video organization tool that helps identify duplicates, similar media, screenshots, and low-quality photos, with compression and cleanup assistance.

开发与运营主体：无锡一己之力信息技术有限责任公司 / Wuxi Yijizhili Information Technology Co., Ltd.
